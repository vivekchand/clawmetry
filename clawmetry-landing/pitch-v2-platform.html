The Problem

Every agent framework has the same blind spot

Whether you use OpenClaw, NanoClaw, PicoClaw, Claude Code, or Cursor, the problem is identical: your agents run autonomously, and you have zero visibility into what they are doing, what they cost, or whether they are working correctly. Each framework has its own log format, its own state management, its own gaps.

Fragmented tooling
