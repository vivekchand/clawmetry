Strategy Guide

How to use these decks

🎯 First Meeting with a VC

Start with V1 (OpenClaw-First) if they know the OSS/AI ecosystem, or V4 (Growth & Metrics) if they are generalists who want numbers first.

📈 Follow-up / Deep Dive

After initial interest, send V3 (Product Depth) for technical due diligence or V2 (Platform) for vision-oriented conversations about where the company goes next.

🤖 Technical Angels / CTOs

Lead with V3 (Product Depth). Technical investors care about moat, architecture, and security. The zero-knowledge E2E encryption story is compelling to this audience.

🏢 Enterprise / Infrastructure VCs

Lead with V2 (Multi-Claw Platform). The Datadog parallel is immediately legible to infrastructure investors. The "start with one, expand to all" playbook is proven.
