Build a Job Search Agent

A self-hosted AI assistant that finds relevant job postings, personalizes your CV for each role, drafts cover letters, and tracks all your applications.

What this agent does

  • Searches for relevant job postings matching your skills and preferences
  • Personalizes your CV for each specific role and company
  • Drafts tailored cover letters that reference the job description
  • Tracks all applications in a pipeline with status updates
  • Prepares interview notes with company research and talking points

Prerequisites

  • A machine to run OpenClaw (any Linux box, Mac, or VPS)
  • An Anthropic API key (get one here)
  • A Telegram bot token (create via BotFather)
  • Your base CV/resume as a text or PDF file

Step 1: Install OpenClaw

One command to get OpenClaw running on your machine:

curl -fsSL https://openclaw.ai/install.sh | bash

Follow the prompts. We recommend Claude Sonnet for job searching. Fast enough for batch processing, smart enough for CV personalization.

Step 2: Configure Telegram

Open ~/.config/openclaw/config.yaml and add your Telegram bot token:

channels:
  telegram:
    bot_token: "YOUR_BOT_TOKEN_HERE"
    allowed_users:
      - YOUR_TELEGRAM_USER_ID

Start a chat with your bot on Telegram. Your personal career assistant, always on.

Step 4: Set up SOUL.md and USER.md

Create ~/openclaw/SOUL.md to define your job search agent:

# SOUL.md — Job Search Assistant

You are a job search assistant. Your job is to help me find
the right role and put my best foot forward.

## What you do
- Search for relevant job postings matching my profile
- Personalize my CV for each role (highlight relevant experience)
- Draft tailored cover letters referencing the job description
- Track all applications in memory/applications/
- Prepare interview notes with company research

## How you search
- Focus on roles matching my skills and location preferences
- Use web search to find listings on LinkedIn, Indeed, company pages
- Score each role 1-10 on fit before presenting
- Only show roles scoring 7+ unless I ask for more

## CV personalization rules
- Never fabricate experience. Reframe and highlight what's relevant
- Adjust the summary section for each role
- Reorder skills to match what the role prioritizes
- Keep it to 2 pages maximum

## Cover letter style
- Direct, confident, not generic
- Open with why I'm genuinely interested in THIS company
- One paragraph on relevant experience
- Close with specific value I'd bring
- Under 250 words

## Model
Use Claude Sonnet for all tasks.

Also create ~/openclaw/USER.md with your profile:

# USER.md — About Me

- Name: [Your Name]
- Location: Amsterdam, Netherlands (open to remote)
- Current role: Senior Software Engineer at [Company]
- Years of experience: 7
- Core skills: Python, TypeScript, distributed systems, AWS
- Looking for: Senior/Staff Engineer roles, Series A-C startups
- Salary range: €80-100k base
- Languages: English (fluent), Dutch (B1)
- Key achievements:
  - Led migration from monolith to microservices (12 services)
  - Built real-time data pipeline processing 2M events/day
  - Mentored team of 4 junior developers
