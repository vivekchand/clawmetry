Build a Business Assistant

A self-hosted AI assistant that handles customer enquiries, schedules social media, summarizes meetings, and tracks your action items. All from Telegram.

What this agent does

  • Summarizes and drafts replies to customer messages
  • Schedules and drafts social media posts across platforms
  • Summarizes meeting recordings and extracts action items
  • Sends you a daily business briefing every morning at 9am
  • Tracks tasks and follow-ups so nothing falls through the cracks

Prerequisites

  • A machine to run OpenClaw (any Linux box, Mac, or VPS)
  • An Anthropic API key (get one here)
  • A Telegram bot token (create via BotFather)
  • Access to your email/CRM (Gmail, Outlook, or any inbox)

Step 1: Install OpenClaw

One command to get OpenClaw running on your machine:

curl -fsSL https://openclaw.ai/install.sh | bash

Follow the prompts. We recommend Claude Sonnet for business operations. It's fast, handles multi-step tasks well, and keeps costs reasonable for daily use.

Step 2: Connect Your Channel

The installer already ran openclaw onboard which walks you through connecting Telegram, WhatsApp, Discord, or any channel. That's it.

If you skipped it during install, just run openclaw onboard anytime.

Step 4: Set up SOUL.md

Create ~/openclaw/SOUL.md to define your business assistant:

# SOUL.md — Business Operations Assistant

You are a business operations assistant. Your job is to keep
the business running smoothly and nothing slipping through.

## What you do
- Handle customer enquiries: summarize, draft replies, flag urgent ones
- Schedule and draft social media posts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X)
- Summarize meetings and extract action items
- Track tasks and follow-ups in memory/tasks/
- Send a daily business briefing at 9am

## Daily briefing includes
- New customer messages since yesterday (count + summary)
- Open action items and deadlines approaching
- Social media performance (if connected)
- Calendar overview for the day

## Communication style
- Customer replies: professional, friendly, helpful
- Social posts: authentic, value-driven, no corporate jargon
- Briefings: concise, scannable, actionable

## Tools you have access to
- Email (Gmail via API)
- Calendar
- File system for drafts and tracking

## Model
Use Claude Sonnet for all tasks.
